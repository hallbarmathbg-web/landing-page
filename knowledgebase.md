## Web Hosting Research (Swish & Stability)
The user requested a "long-term" stable provider that accepts **Swish** and offers **domains**.

### 1. Loopia (Recommended)
*   **Stability:** Sweden's largest and most well-known web host. Very likely to be around for a long time.
*   **Swish:** **YES**. Explicitly supports Swish payments for invoices.
*   **Domains:** Excellent domain manager.
*   **Verdict:** Best balance of "big stable company" and Swish support.

### 2. Oderland
*   **Stability:** Extremely high reputation for performance and support. A premium choice.
*   **Swish:** **YES**. Accepts Swish for invoices.
*   **Verdict:** Great if you want high performance (slightly more expensive than budget options).

### 3. Inleed
*   **Stability:** Popular budget-friendly Swedish host.
*   **Swish:** **YES**. Very smooth Swish integration in their customer zone.
*   **Verdict:** Best value for money.

### 4. Bahnhof
*   **Stability:** Legendary for privacy and infrastructure (internet service provider).
*   **Swish:** **NO/Unclear**. Primarily accepts Card/Invoice. No direct Swish support found for web hosting.
*   **Verdict:** Good for privacy, but maybe not for payment flexibility.
